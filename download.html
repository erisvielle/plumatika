Release Date

September 14, 2025

Introduction to Plumatika

Plumatika is an AI-powered Baybayin learning app designed to help you learn Baybayin through structured lessons, guided handwriting practice, interactive quizzes, and mini games. Progress through chapters, complete quizzes, and earn achievements. Plumatika makes Baybayin learning engaging through repetition, instant feedback, and gamification. Create your account, verify your email, and start your journey to mastering Baybayin!

Key Features:

  • Structured Lessons & Quizzes: Handwriting exercises and multiple-choice questions guide your learning.
  • Guided Handwriting Practice: Write each character stroke by stroke and get instant feedback. Practice as many times as you want.
  • Mini Games: Typing Challenge and Offline Flashcards to make learning fun.
  • Dashboard & Leaderboards: Track your progress and see how you rank among other learners.
