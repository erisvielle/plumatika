Frequently Asked Questions

Installation

Download the .apk file from the Plumatika official website and install it on your Android device.

On Android, go to Settings > Security > Install unknown apps (or similar depending on your device) and allow your browser or file manager to install apps from unknown sources.

Plumatika supports most modern Android devices. Make sure your device is reasonably up to date to ensure smooth performance.

Plumatika requires approximately 130 MB of free storage for installation, plus additional space for your progress and data.

Yes, an internet connection is required to download the .apk file.


Account Setup and Management

Open the app, click Sign Up, enter your email and password, and follow the prompts to create your account. If account is existing, press Login.

You will receive a verification code via email submitted. Enter it in the app to complete verification.

Not in this version. Only email and password are supported for account creation.

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and follow the instructions.

Open the Dashboard, tap your profile, and edit your information.


App Navigation and Usage

Open the menu drawer on the top left of the screen and toggle between English and Filipino.

Open the menu drawer on the top left of the screen and tap Sign Out.

To access the full app, an internet connection is required. Offline Flashcards is the only feature that can be accessed without internet connection.


Troubleshooting and Support

Restart the app. If the problem persists, try clearing the app cache in your device settings or reinstalling the .apk file.

Use the Help Guide in the app or contact support via email. Include details about your device and the issue. You can also share your experience through the feedback survey located at the top right of the app header. Do note that this survey can only be taken once in this version.

Email the support team at plumatika.ming@gmail.com.


Privacy and Data

Plumatika encrypts sensitive information and follows standard data privacy practices.

Plumatika collects basic account information, quiz answers and results, and feedback to improve the app and for research purposes.

Contact support via email (plumatika.ming@gmail.com) to request deletion of your account and all associated data.


Updates and Feedback

First, delete the old .apk version from your device to avoid installation errors. Download the newest .apk from the official website and install it.

You can give feedback and suggestions through answering the survey in the app, however do note that this survey can only be taken once in this version. You may also email your suggestions to plumatika.ming@gmail.com.
